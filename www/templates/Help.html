What is Ebolapp

Ebolapp is an application created by WHO team to help healthcare worker quickly notice and quarantine an ebola outbreak using the data provided by civillians of an infected area.


The Idea

  • The app will display the location of the user Google Map style so you can see the area around your location as well
  • The user can tap on alert button which will mark a red dot on the map at their location and other users can also see it
  • The healthcare worker using their version of the app can see which area is very red and come investigate
  • The app also provide advice about avoiding the area and guidelines about ebola prevention

This app is created to serve as a visual demonstration of the app idea for UCL Global Citizenship Program.
This is by no mean the final look and feel of the app, many features are not included in this version.
